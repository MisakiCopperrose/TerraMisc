Changelog
---------
0.14.0 (0.79.29)

-Tweak: Road Blocks are now refered to as Tiles, this is just a change made in the lang file.
-Fix: Size and Weight information not being displayed on the Sugar Bottle Item.
-Fix: ToolTips/WAILA information now localizes on the Quiver, Crossbow, and Candles.
-Added: Source code to Github. https://github.com/Powerman913717/terramisc
-Added: A Wet Clay Block, can be baked near open flames or lava source blocks, to produce hardened clay.
-Added:	Breaking wild pumpkins no longer drops a block but instead drops food. (Wild Pumpkins yeild around 10 ounces)
		-Pumpkin blocks can be placed from pumpkin food items at a rate of 80 ounces per block.
		-Pumpkin blocks can then be carved with knives. (Right=Click)
		-Carved Pumpkins can become Jack O'Lanterns with the additon of a candle. (Right-Click)
-Added: Cayenne Peppers, Coffee, Watermelon, Hops, and Sweet Potato crops.
-Added: Bears now drop a meat item, which can be cooked and used in meals.
-Added: Right-Clicking a log pile only containing a single log with 15 additional logs of the same type will completely fill the pile.
-Added: Lead Pewter Alloy, a Tin-Copper-Lead Alloy.
-Added: Gold, Silver, and Lead Pewter Casings. All require an ingot of their respective material and an anvil of copper or better.
-Tweak: Compass recipe no longer accepts Wrought Iron Casings instead Lead Pewter or Silver Casings must be used.
